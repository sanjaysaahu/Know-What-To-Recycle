Recyclable!!

How to recycle?

Aluminium cans are shredded, removing any coloured coating. They are then melted in a huge furnace and the molten metal is poured into ingot casts to set. Each ingot can be made into around 1.5 million cans. Aluminium foil is a different alloy and is usually recycled separately with other aluminium scraps to make cast items such as engine components.
